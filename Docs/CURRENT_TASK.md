## Objective

Review and accept the completed S0B branch, then decide whether to define and approve a later development batch.

S0B completion does not approve S1 or any product implementation.

## Scope

- Review the S0B commit, diff, build evidence, Unit Test evidence, repeated UI smoke-test evidence, standard-launch observation, and forbidden-item checks.
- Confirm that the App remains a static placeholder and that Macro Stage S0 is complete.
- Decide the next batch boundary and issue separate explicit execution approval if further work should begin.

## Constraints

- Do not start S1 or make product changes without a new explicit Owner-approved task.
- Do not treat acceptance of S0B as approval of any later stage.
- Preserve the Foundation Documents and Development Contract unless a separately approved governance change requires otherwise.

## Success Criteria

- Owner reviews the completed S0B evidence and records acceptance or requested corrections.
- If continued development is desired, the next batch receives an explicit objective, scope, constraints, validation, exit criteria, and execution approval.

## Out of Scope

- S1 task design or implementation.
- Any product domain, persistence, media, navigation, or feature work.
- Merging to `main` before Owner Review.
